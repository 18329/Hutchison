Random facts about leopard geckos

This is a picture of a leopard gecko

-Leopard geckos can live for over 20 years in captivity.

-They are insectivores, and eat mainly mealworms and crickets.

-They can grow up to 30cm long.

-They are one of the most popular pet reptiles in New Zealand.

-There are many colour variants of leopard geckos, with the main colour being yellow/black.
